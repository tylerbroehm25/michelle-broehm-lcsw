About Michelle

Honest, warm support — with a bit of humor

If you've been feeling stuck, repeating patterns you can't seem to break, or just emotionally drained — you're in the right place. I've spent over a decade helping adults and older adults move through anxiety, ADHD, life transitions, and more.

My approach is grounded in psychodynamic and social learning theory, using evidence-based tools like ACT, CBT, and motivational interviewing to help you understand your values, your behavior patterns, and how to take action that actually aligns with who you want to be.

I believe therapy should feel real, not clinical. If you don't mind a bit of honesty and the occasional laugh, I'd love to connect for a free 15-minute consultation.
